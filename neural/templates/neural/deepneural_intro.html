Why We Chose AI for Healthcare

Healthcare and medicine are one of the most vital aspects of human civilization. Throughout the centuries - and most recently - medicine has allowed humanity to achieve wonders the likes of which were unheard of in ages of old; we were able to discover cures for the most life-threathening of diseases such as - but not limited to - Cholera, Malaria, and more recently, Covid-19. The aforementioned examples mark a minor percentage of the total amount of illnesses humanity has successfully been able to overcome. However, the fight for a healthier species is still far out of reach. Recent advancements in artificial intelligence, and more specifically, machine learning, have - and will continue to - catalyze medical breakthroughs. Our mission therefore, is to be a part of that future; a world where we as a species can thrive, knowing that we have successfully found the cure to most, if not all, viral, bacterial, and cancerous illnesses.

At it's core, DeepNeural is, in its current state, a start-up project seeking to develop a plethora of machine learning models - ranging from classical ML models (tabular), to natural language and computer vision - with the goal of aiding medical professionals and their respective patients, ensuring faster diagnostics of various illnesses, to promote a greater well-being for those who are truly in need, in a quick period of time; nothing is more important than the health of a patient, and DeepNeural seeks to work with that statement in mind.

This however, is not where this project for better healthcare meets a halt; the healthcare sector provides a great amount of flexibility for expansion; medical robotics, assistive chatbots for mental well-being, and neural interfaces capable of providing those individuals who are paraplegic, the ability to live their lives to the fullest once more; this is DeepNeural's overall vision, and although the project is still in its infancy, that vision, will be attained. Humanity's health, happiness, and prosperity is our top priority.
